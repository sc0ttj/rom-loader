You play the role of Landau, a man who must pass three tests in order to gain the crown of the land Baljinya, which for years has been rife with lawlessness and fear, and restore peace to the land. To pass the three tests, you must find the Tree of Marill, subdue the Goblin of Balala Valley, and destroy the Statue Of Evil.

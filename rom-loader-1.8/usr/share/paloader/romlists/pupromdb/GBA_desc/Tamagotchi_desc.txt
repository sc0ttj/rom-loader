Now there's a whole new way to experience all of the fun and excitement of America's favorite digital pet with Tamagotchi for your Nintendo Game Boy. With the Game Boy version, you can raise up to 3 Tamagotchi at one time. You can change your Tamagotchi's growth rate, save unfinished games so that you can return to them later and enter your Tamagotchi in tournaments! There are also more ways to care for your Tamagotchi than with the original toy!

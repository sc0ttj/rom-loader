Two of your favorite episodes from the Sonic the Hedgehog animated series arrive on the Game Boy Advance. Sonic X: Volume 1 includes "Chaos Control Freaks" and "Sonic to the Rescue." In "Chaos Control Freaks," Dr. Eggman blasts Sonic into a world where Sonic is the target of a high-speed police squad. In "Sonic to the Rescue," Sonic and Chuck attempt to rescue Cream and Cheese from a top secret military base filled with booby traps.

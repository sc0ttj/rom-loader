Go Pot-Holing on a magical journey through the complex underground tunnels and caves that are Cavernia.

In your search for the precious artifacts and gems in each level, you will come up against a series of intricate puzzles, treacherous mutants, static electric fields, dangerous rock movements, and lots, lots, more!!

Cavernia is a fast moving and deadly game of speed, skill and nerves.

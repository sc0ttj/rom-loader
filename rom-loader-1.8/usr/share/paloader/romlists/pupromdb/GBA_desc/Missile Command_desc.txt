Two classic arcade games on one pak:

Asteroids: Pilot a spacecraft and destroy the on-coming asteroids, with a range of different weapons and shields.

Missile Command: Command missile launchers and protect the planet from asteroids, space ships and other missiles.

Both are complete remakes of the arcade originals.

http://www.mobygames.com/game/gameboy/arcade-classic-1-asteroids-missile-command
Missile Command is a Shoot-'Em-Up game, developed by The Code Monkeys and published by Hasbro Interactive, which was released in 1999.

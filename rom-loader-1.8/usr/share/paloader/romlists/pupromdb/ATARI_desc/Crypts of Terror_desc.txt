Get the magic ring of power back to crypt #1. You explore various crypts on a map, fighting monsters, looking for the ring. A secret will be revealed to those that complete the quest at the highest difficulty level.

Tips:
Type MAGIC when requested for first level keyword.

Loading instructions: 
- type CLOAD 
- type RUN.

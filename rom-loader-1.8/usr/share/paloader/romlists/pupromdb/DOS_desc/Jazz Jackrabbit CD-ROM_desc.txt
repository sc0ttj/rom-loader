This special CD-ROM edition of Jazz Jackrabbit contains the original six episodes, plus three new ones. These episodes ("Outta Dis World", "Wild Wabbit", and "Turtle Soup") has Jazz exploring planets with such names as Exoticus, Industrius, Deserto, Stonar, and several others. The episode feature new music, new graphics, more enemies, and new bosses.

The screenshots included in this entry were taken from the three new episodes. They are not from the original episodes. These can be found here.

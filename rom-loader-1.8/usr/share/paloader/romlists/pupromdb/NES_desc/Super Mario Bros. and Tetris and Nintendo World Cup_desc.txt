Combo cart containing three Nintendo classics on one single cartridge. Super Mario Bros. stars "Jump Man" himself, Mario, along with his brother Luigi, and takes players through more than 30 colorful levels of varied theme and design, all of them packed with cute, stylized enemies like the goombas and a wide array of platforms and obstacles. Tetris is a faithful console adaptation of Pajitnov's insanely addictive puzzle game, and World Cup is a Technos developed soccer game.

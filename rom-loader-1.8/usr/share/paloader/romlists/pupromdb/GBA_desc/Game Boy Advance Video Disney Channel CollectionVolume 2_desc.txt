Disney Channel Collection Vol. 2 lets you watch Lilo & Stitch, Brandy & Mr. Whiskers, and Disney's Kim Possible on your Game Boy Advance. With the included three episodes, you can join miniature versions of Lilo and Stitch as they explore Pleakley's alien insides; you can watch as Mr. Whiskers accidentally sends himself to space; and you can find out if Kim Possible's dog is good enough to win a dog show.

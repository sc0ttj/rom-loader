A 2d beat em up, that plays like Street Fighter 2, featuring playable characters such as Homer Simpson, Bart Simpson, Family Guys Peter Griffin, Futuramas Fry, Bender & Leela.. and more!

Press F1 for help when it loads up.

This is custom made MUGEN game, using various sources, put together by sc0ttman for Puppy Linux.

There is a file called gmugen in the cartoon_fighter folder, which lets you change settings.. This should also be available in the Menu->Fun

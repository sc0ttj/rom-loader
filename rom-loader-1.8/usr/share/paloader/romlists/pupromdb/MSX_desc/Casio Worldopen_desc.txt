Casio's World Golf Open features an 18 hole golf course in 3D and it allows you to choose the right club out of several driver, iron and putter golf clubs.
Further, you can adjust your stance, the strength of the stroke and control your swing.
All information can be found in the status bar at the bottom of the screen.
The game can be played by one or two players.

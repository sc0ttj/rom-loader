Look up in the stars! It's Astro Grover and he's all set to have some close encounters with the friendly Zips from the planet Zap. Children practive counting, adding, and subtracting skill as they help Grover count the Zips and welcome them to earth! Children help Grover count the Zips and if they are right, Grover will fly out to meet them. In case of a wrong answer, the Man-in-the-Moon will shake his head and encourage the child to try again. As children develop their number skills, a series of correct answers results in the building of Grover's beautiful city or the launching of a zippy spaceship.

~From the back of the box

In Spirits & Spells, you can choose to play the role of Alicia, a girl disguised as a witch, or Greg, a boy disguised as a devil, as they attempt to rescue their friends from a mad scientist. You can alternate between the two characters at any time during the game and discover their varying abilities. Explore creepy environments such as a haunted house, a dark cemetery, eerie forests, and an abandoned laboratory. To survive this Halloween world, you must solve puzzles and fight a variety of evil creatures.
